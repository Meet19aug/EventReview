Apple Event 2021

Big updates regarding the AirTag 2021.

  1. Now You can personalize your AirTag and add beautiful accessories from apple design team.
  2. precision finding uses apples U1 chip to get precise distance to your AirTag.
  3. Iphone fuses data with input from camera,accelerometer and gyroscope to give you visual, haptic and audible feedback.
  4. AirTag is designed to track items,not people so apple adds feature of unwanted tag detection, Rotating identifiers, Audible alerts.
  5. AirTag price is starts from $29, and 4 pack of Airtag is for $99.
  6. AirTag will be available from April 30.
