Apple Event 2021

Big updates regarding the new iPad Pro 2021.

  1. Apple switched from “Turbo Charged versions of their A-series chips” to their latest “M1 processor”, used in the latest MacBooks.
  2. Support for the latest Xbox and play station game controllers.
  3. Up to 2Tb storage.
  4. Now supports 5G To provide ultra-fast wireless connection it. (in the US it even supports millimeter wave, which means in ideal conditions it can reach up to 4GB of download speeds)
  5. Pro cameras with motion tracking, excellent low light photo quality, all-new front camera with “12 MP Ultra Wide Camera and center stage”.
  6. “Liquid Retina XDR” with 1000 nits full-screen brightness, 1600 nits peak brightness, and 1000000:1 contrast ratio which is just amazing for the professional content creators.
  7. iPad Pro(11-inch) starts at $799.
  8. iPad Pro(12.9-inch) starts at $1099.
